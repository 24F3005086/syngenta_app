🧠 AI Algorithms & Pattern Extraction

Multi-Factor Health Score (Weighted Composite)

Sales Score — 30 pts
Inventory Score — 25 pts
Campaign Score — 25 pts
Visit Score — 20 pts

Churn Prediction (Rule-Based Scoring)

  • Inactivity >21 days → +35 risk
  • Sales drop >30% MoM → +30 risk
  • No field visits in 90 days → +25 risk

5 Extracted Patterns

1️⃣ Supply Chain Risk — Inventory stockout × POS velocity cross-reference

2️⃣ Crop Lifecycle Demand — Grower calendar → dominant crop → product match

3️⃣ Marketing-to-Retail Link — WhatsApp clicks → retailer restock urgency

4️⃣ Churn Early Warning — Sales decline + visit absence = risk flag

5️⃣ Digital Funnel — 3.8M impressions → 74K visits → 4.9K leads

🖼️ CNN — Crop Disease Detection (Future Scope)

Architecture: MobileNetV2 Transfer Learning

📸 Input: Leaf Image (224×224×3)
🔽 MobileNetV2 Base (Pre-trained on ImageNet)
🔽 Global Average Pooling
🔽 Dense(256, ReLU) + Dropout(0.5)
🔽 Dense(38, Softmax) → Disease Class
✅ Output: Disease Name → Syngenta Product Match

Integration Flow

📷 Grower captures leaf photo via mobile

🧠 CNN classifies disease (e.g., Wheat Rust, Leaf Blight)

💊 System matches disease → Syngenta product from catalog

📍 Nearest retailer with that product in stock is recommended

Training Details

ParameterValue
DatasetPlantVillage (54K images)
Classes38 disease categories
FrameworkTensorFlow / Keras
Accuracy~96% (transfer learning)
